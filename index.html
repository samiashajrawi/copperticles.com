The Most Oxidation-Stable Copper Ink

Copperticles produces copper ink that can withstand oxidation. The ink can be screen-printed in the ambient atmosphere. There is no need for a reducing gas/atmosphere. The ink does not need severe storage conditions. It can simply be stored under a vacuum.

Applications

Market:

The market for copper conductive ink is wide with a huge variety in different applications. Currently, there is an unmet need for oxidation-stable copper ink that can provide good conductivity

Substrate Diversity

Our ink is suitable for a variety of substrates like PET, PEN, PI, Kapton, paper, and more

High Conductivity

Sheet resistance is as low as 59.5mΩ/□/10micron ( 23.8mΩ/□/mil ) with cheap heat-sensitive substrates like PET
